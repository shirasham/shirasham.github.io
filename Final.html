A Look at Anti-Jewish Hate Crimes Between the Years 1991 and 2019¶

Introduction¶

The last few years have seen a rise in reporting of Anti-Jewish hate crimes, ranging from swasticas in grafitti, verbal harrasment and mass shooting. There have been a number of troubleing mass shootings targeting Jews and Jewish institutions resulting many deaths. The shooting at the Tree of Life synagogue in 2018 left 11 dead and sent shock waves through American Jewish communitty, with may feeling that they were also valnerable agianst such an attack (https://www.vox.com/the-highlight/2019/10/18/20899208/tree-of-life-anniversary-pittsburgh-shooting-american-jews). As an American Jew I want to explore what exactly has been happening in terms of Anti-Jewish hate crime in the U.S. to see if these crimes are really becoming more prevalent of if its just that they are being reported on more often then before. I'm going to bring you along to show you the Data Science Pipeline.

I want to clean up the data even more to make it easier to catagorize incidnets later on. I specifically want to make changes to the "OFFENDER_ETHNICITY" which is overwhemingly poppulated with "Nan", which means that the column does not apply to that incident. If we combine "OFFENDER_ETHNICITY" with "OFFENDER_RACE" when ethnicity isn't nan then we can more easily characterize the Offender in when want to later on.

Additionally, there is a lot of options to catigorize the type of victim, as seen above. As a result I want to focuse on only a couple of those options and classify the others as "other". I specifically want to look at the "individual" and "Religious Organizaion" types. I'm going to make a nw column in the "anti_jewish_hc" data table to store my new classification of victim type which I'm going to call "VICIM_TYPES2". To fill this new colunm we are going to iterate throught the data frame and anytime the "VICTIM_TYPES" is at least partially "Individual" or "Religious Organization" I am going to set "VICTIM_TYPES2" to "Individual" or "Religious Organiation" respectivly.
